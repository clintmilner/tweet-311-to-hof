311 has one of the most impressive presences in the music world. Together, with all 5 original members for 29 years now, 311 is still going hard and putting on endless tours, events and festivals to keep the vibe and rocking going with their colossal fan base. 311 radiates positivity in to their fans lives. Legions of fans world wide travel to amazing places to take part in the phenomenon that is 311. To 311 cruises and 311 Day, this group of men has single handedly created a holiday for their fans where they pay US homage.  

311 is one of those bands who not only cares for their fans but makes them a part of their lives. After live shows, you can catch them by the tour buses where they are there simply to connect with "The Excitables". Pictures, laughs and memories are made and that simple kind gesture has set them aside from the rest. They network with their fans on social media, including us on various projects such as the cover art for their last album, Mosaic. There is a togetherness that 311 brings to us.

These 5 men are hard working and and inspirational and steadily finding new ways to exude their natural, God given talents. Every show is met with a new playlist. Every show. the devotion that they put into their music, performances and presence is unmatched by any. They are the fourth longest running band for a reason. The passion and genius that is 311 is far beyond worthy of an accolade such as being inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame. As 311 said in their song Too Late, "at the end of the day, the relentless always win". That is the whole vibe of 311. Never quit. Never stop. Believe in yourself. Stay Positive and Love your Life.

Lets get these immensely talented men inducted in to the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame as a HUGE THANK YOU to the love and unity that they have brought into millions of our lives!!
